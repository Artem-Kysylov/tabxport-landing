Google Sheets is great for data analysis, but when it comes to presenting that data professionally, you need clean PDF reports. This guide will show you how to transform your spreadsheet data into presentation-ready documents.

## Why Export to PDF?

PDF reports offer several advantages over sharing raw spreadsheets:

- **Professional appearance** for client presentations
- **Consistent formatting** across devices and platforms  
- **Print-ready** documents with proper page breaks
- **Brand consistency** with logos and custom styling
- **Security** - recipients can't accidentally modify your data

## Step 1: Prepare Your Google Sheets Data

Before exporting, ensure your data is presentation-ready:

### Clean Up Your Data
- Remove empty rows and columns
- Ensure consistent formatting
- Add clear headers and labels
- Round numbers to appropriate decimal places

### Organize for PDF Layout
- Keep related data together
- Consider page width limitations
- Add summary rows where helpful

## Step 2: Export from Google Sheets

1. **Copy your data range** (including headers)
2. Visit [TableXport.com](https://tablexport.com)  
3. **Paste** your data into the input area
4. Select **PDF** as your export format

## Step 3: Customize Your PDF

### Add Professional Branding
- Upload your company logo (Pro feature)
- Choose from predefined templates
- Set consistent fonts and colors

### Optimize Layout
- **Portrait vs Landscape**: Choose based on your data width
- **Page breaks**: Ensure logical separation
- **Headers and footers**: Add page numbers and dates

## Advanced PDF Features

### Custom Styling
- **Font selection**: Choose professional typefaces
- **Color schemes**: Match your brand guidelines
- **Table borders**: Clean, professional appearance

### Multiple Tables
- Combine related datasets
- Add explanatory text between tables
- Create comprehensive reports

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it simple**: Don't overcrowd pages
2. **Use white space**: Improve readability
3. **Consistent formatting**: Professional appearance
4. **Clear labels**: Make data self-explanatory

Your Google Sheets data is now transformed into a professional PDF report, ready for presentations or client delivery!
